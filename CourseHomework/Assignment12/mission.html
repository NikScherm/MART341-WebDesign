About Our Mission

At Ma's and Pa's Giftshop, our mission is to bring joy and warmth into every home through unique, thoughtfully crafted items that celebrate life’s special moments. We believe that every gift has the power to create memories, express love, and share joy, whether it’s a small token of appreciation or a personalized keepsake. Rooted in our values of community, craftsmanship, and authenticity, we carefully curate a selection of handmade, locally sourced, and eco-friendly products that reflect our commitment to quality and care. Ma’s and Pa’s is more than just a store; it’s a welcoming space where customers can find gifts that speak from the heart and bring people closer together.



About the Owners

Meredith Ann Lenley and Bruce Lenley, a couple in their late 50s, have always shared a passion for community, creativity, and connection. After decades spent working in corporate roles, they decided to pursue their dream of opening Ma's and Pa's Giftshop to combine their love for thoughtful gifts with their desire to serve their community. Inspired by cherished family traditions and the joy of giving, Meredith and Bruce wanted to create a shop that celebrates life's milestones and little moments alike. They believe that a gift shop should be a place where everyone feels welcome, whether they're browsing for the perfect birthday present or simply enjoying a stroll through the aisles of beautiful, one-of-a-kind items. For Meredith and Bruce, Ma's and Pa's is more than a business; it’s a heartfelt expression of their belief in the importance of shared experiences and the joy of finding just the right gift.
